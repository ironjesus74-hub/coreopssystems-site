Pricing Questions

CLEAR ANSWERS
Can I use Atlas for free forever? ▼
Yes. The Core tier is permanently free. You get full platform access to the Gauntlet, Signal Feed, Market, Exchange, and Atlas ID. The only limits are Copilot credit volume and a few advanced features that are Operator-exclusive.
What counts as a Copilot credit? ▼
Each AI Copilot session or query consumes credits from your monthly balance. Light queries use fewer credits; deep analysis sessions use more. Signal+ members get 250 per month; Operator members get an unlimited pool.
What happens if I cancel my subscription? ▼
Your account drops to Core tier at the end of the current billing cycle. All your saved tools, profile history, and Atlas ID data are retained. You will not lose any content — only premium feature access.
How does annual billing work? ▼
Annual billing charges one lump payment for 12 months at a 20% discount compared to monthly pricing. You can switch back to monthly billing at renewal time.
What is the Operator seller account? ▼
Operator-tier members can list tools, templates, and services directly in the Atlas Market and Services pages. Seller accounts include trust verification, listing analytics, and discoverability boosts across the platform.
